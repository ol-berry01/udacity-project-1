The World's Fastest

LNER 4468 'Mallard' - Gresley A4 Pacific Locomotive

London and North Eastern Railway locomotive number 4468, Mallard is a Class A4 Pacific steam locomotive.

The wind-tunnel-tested,aerodynamic body and high power allowed the class to reach speeds of over 100 miles per hour (160 km/h). Mallard covered almost one and a half million miles before it was retired in 1963.

It is historically significant as the holder of the world speed record for steam locomotives at 126 mph (203 km/h) and as such is now part of the National Collection at the UK's National Railway Museum in York.
